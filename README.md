# Chico's FAS (chicos-fas)

Chico's FAS, Inc. is an American women's specialty retailer headquartered in Fort Myers, Florida, operating and franchising stores and websites under the Chico's, White House Black Market, and Soma brands. The company is a leading omni-channel specialty retailer of private branded sophisticated, casual-to-dressy clothing, intimates, sleepwear, and related accessories, with more than 1,400 retail and outlet stores in the United States and a substantial e-commerce footprint. Chico's FAS is part of the KnitWell Group portfolio.
